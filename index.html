About KSF

One community,
many stories.

Komunitas Solidaritas Furry (KSF) is a gathering space for Indonesian furries from those just discovering the furry world to long time members of the fandom.

We believe everyone deserves to express themselves freely, safely, and with dignity. KSF is here to bridge connections, support local artists, and build a positive space for all.

Solidarity Above All

We support each other, respect our differences, and look out for one another within the community.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is furry and what is KSF?
Furry is a fandom centered around anthropomorphic animal characters. KSF (Komunitas Solidaritas Furry) is an Indonesian furry community focused on inclusivity, solidarity, and mutual support among its members.
Do I need a fursona to join?
Not at all! You can join even without a fursona. Many of our members are still in the process of creating their first character — and this community is here to help!
What platform does KSF use?
We're currently active on WhatsApp. Going forward, we'll be developing our own platform at kosofurr.dev with more complete features for the Indonesian furry community.
Is this community safe for all ages?
KSF is an all-ages friendly community. We have strict content rules, and our active moderation team ensures the environment stays safe and comfortable for all members.
How can I contribute to KSF?
You can contribute by being active in the WhatsApp group, sharing artwork, helping with moderation, or even joining platform development. Reach out to our admins for more info!
